## Dialogue Practice
**Activist:** What steps can individuals take to reduce their carbon footprint?

**Expert:** Simple actions like recycling, using public transport, and conserving energy can make a big difference.

**Activist:** How can we encourage more people to adopt sustainable habits?

**Expert:** Education and community initiatives are effective ways to promote environmental responsibility.

## Article
Environmental issues are a global concern. At the C1 level, individuals should be able to discuss causes, effects, and solutions to problems like climate change and pollution. Understanding scientific concepts, evaluating policies, and advocating for change require advanced language skills. Collaboration and innovation are key to creating a sustainable future.
